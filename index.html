Holiday Pay Calculator

A Holiday Pay Calculator designed specifically for United Flight Attendants!

About the App:

Holiday Pay Calculator is a great tool for United Flight Attendants!

This new tool incorporates our new formula for holiday pay in a user friendly format. Simply enter four values: Hourly Pay Rate, Block Hours for the Trip, Time Away From Base for the trip, and Hours on Holiday. The app does the rest, and displays your total Holiday Pay!

This app will be continuously supported despite being available for a ONE time fee! The embedded formula will be updated as changes are made to it, and these update will come at NO additional charge.
